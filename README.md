# FirstOne – Earn Your Scrool Time

 Work to earn coins. Spend coins to unlock procastinating sites. Gamified focus that actually works.
## How It Works
You open Chrome and browse normally. Every **10 minutes** of active browsing on any non-blocked site earns you **15 coins**. Want to visit Instagram or TikTok? You gotta pay for it.

 # Action and their Result 

 Active on any non-blocked site ---> +15 coins every 10 mins 
 Try to open a blocked site ---> Lock screen appears 
 Spend 20 coins---> 10 mins unlocked on that site 
 Coins run out mid-session--->Warning shown, then blocked 
 Switch to another app--->Timer pauses 
 Midnight(local browser time)---> All coins reset, fresh start

## IMPORTANT NOTE

- Timer only counts when **Browser is your active window**
- Switching to VS Code, Spotify, or any other app **pauses** the timer
- Coins reset daily at **12am your local time**
- Your data is stored **locally** — nothing is sent anywhere

##  How to Load

1. Go to `chrome://extensions`
2. Enable **Developer Mode** (top right)
3. Click **Load unpacked**
4. Select the `FirstOne/` folder
5. Pin it to your toolbar and you're good 

 **Coin Economy**

 Earn rate = 15 coins per 10 mins worked 
 Unlock cost = 20 coins = 10 mins on blocked site 
 1 hour of work = 90 coins = 45 mins of unlocked time
